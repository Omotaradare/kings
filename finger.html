WELCOME TO KINGS HEART SCHOOLS

MISSSION

Is to enable all learners' access to learning through provision of;

  • Safe and nurturing learning environment, which builds a strong foundation for character development, good morals, godly knowledge and life long learnings, utilizing best educational practices to engage each student in a culturally rich and challenging curriculum.
  • Highly effective teachers, focussed on improving student outcomes,through their committment to ongoing professional development, quality teaching, evidence based on practices, coaching and mentoring and collaboration.
  • A quality indusive learning environment that is responsive to student voice.
  • Enriching and engaging resources.
  • Opportunities for community and parents to participate in learning and decision-making partnership.

OUR VISION

Our vision at kings' heart school is to empower students to demonstate, articulate and acquire knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise our core values.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Expectations of Exccellence -an environment of highly expectations in which all people consistently strive to operate at optimal performance level.
  • Respect -caring for all people and valuing their unconditional worth.
  • Intergrity\Ethical behaviour -Consistent action based on the highest standard of credibility, honesty and ethical behaviour.
    • Collaboration -working together to achieve common goals through open and honest communication.
  • Communication -conveying thoughts and ideas through a variety of mediums that promote clarity and understanding.
